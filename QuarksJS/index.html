Welcome to Quarks!

This is an idle game all about creating, starting from the smallest particles up to galaxies and beyond. No prestige or reset mechanics; just creating.

I don't have a background in physics, chemistry, or astronomy so some of the information might be incorrect. I included notes in many items that I found interesting in an attempt to make this game educational as well as fun.

I tried to keep this game acurrate to reality without getting too complex. The settings can significanly adjust the difficulty and amount of time required for creating larger items.
Click the rainbow elements to get started.
